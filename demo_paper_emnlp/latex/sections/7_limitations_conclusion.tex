\section{Conclusion}

AutoTerm-SST provides zero-setup terminology retrieval over a modular offline
catalog. On mixed ACL and ESO oncology talks, it matches known-domain quality,
beats InfiniSST, avoids 1M universal-glossary noise, and makes this behavior
visible in the demo.

\section*{Limitations}
\label{sec:limitations}

The En$\rightarrow$Zh slices contain benchmark terms, so results measure
retrieval competition rather than unseen-glossary induction. Routing may lag
topic changes, and scale sensitivity depends on language and budgets; 100k and
1M are not universal thresholds. Multilingual validation is future work.
Following RASST, ESO Chinese references are GPT-5.4-generated, not human.
