\subsection{LLM Serving Systems}

The landscape of LLM serving has been transformed by systems like vLLM~\citep{kwon2023efficient} and SGLang~\citep{zheng2023sglang}, which introduce continuous batching and efficient memory management. However, these systems focus on text-based applications and lack the speech-specific optimizations required for SST.

ORCA~\citep{yu2022orca} introduces the concept of separating prefill and decode operations in transformer serving, which we adapt for the unique characteristics of speech translation workloads.
