\section{Conclusion}

AutoTerm-SST provides zero-setup terminology retrieval over a modular offline
catalog. On mixed ACL and ESO oncology talks, it matches known-domain quality,
beats InfiniSST, avoids 1M universal-glossary noise, and makes this behavior
visible in the demo.

\section*{Limitations}
\label{sec:limitations}

The En$\rightarrow$Zh slices contain benchmark terms, so we measure routing and
retrieval competition rather than glossary induction. Routing can lag topic
changes, and scale sensitivity depends on language and budgets; 100k and 1M
are not universal thresholds. Multilingual validation remains future work.
